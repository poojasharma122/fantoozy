Points Leagues

How It Works: Players earn a set number of points for each stat (e.g., 1 pt per rebound, 2 pts per assist, 3 pts per steal).

Pros: Simple, easy to track, mirrors daily box scores.

Cons: Overvalues volume players who fill stat sheets, even if inefficient.

9-Cat Leagues

How It Works: Tracks nine categories (points, rebounds, assists, steals, blocks, FG%, FT%, 3PM, turnovers).

Pros: More strategic, rewards efficiency and well-rounded players.

Cons: Higher learning curve, punishes high-usage players with poor shooting.
