Drafting 101: How to Build Your Fantasy Team

Draft day is the most important part of your season. A strong draft gives you a foundation to succeed, while mistakes can leave you playing catch-up.

Common Draft Formats:

  • Snake Draft: Picks reverse order each round (most popular).
  • Auction Draft: Managers bid on players with a set budget.
  • Keeper/Dynasty Drafts: Keep players from previous seasons for long-term strategy.

Tips for Draft Day Success:

  • Know the Scoring System – Draft based on league format (points, PPR, 9-cat, etc.).
  • Mock Draft Often – Practice builds confidence and exposes value picks.
  • Balance Risk & Safety – Mix star players with consistent mid-tier talent.
  • Draft for Depth – Injuries happen, so build strong benches.
  • Stay Flexible – Don’t lock into one strategy; adapt to the draft flow.

A good draft doesn’t guarantee a championship, but it puts you in the best possible position to succeed.
