Fantasy Sports Terminology Explained

Fantasy has its own language. Here are terms every manager should know:

  • ADP (Average Draft Position): Where players are typically selected in drafts.
  • Waiver Wire: Free agents available to add once games begin.
  • PPR (Points Per Reception): Scoring format where receptions add points (football).
  • 9-Cat / 8-Cat: Basketball scoring systems (turnovers included or not).
  • Sleeper: A player expected to outperform their draft position.
  • Bust: A player who fails to meet expectations.
  • Streaming: Picking up and dropping players frequently to maximize matchups.
  • Handcuff: Drafting a backup to a star (especially RBs in football).

Learning these terms will make guides, forums, and league chat much easier to follow.
