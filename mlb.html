What to Look For in an MLB Fantasy Platform

Choosing the right fantasy baseball site depends on your playstyle:

  • League Size & Format- Head-to-head, rotisserie, or points leagues.
  • Draft Options- Live snake drafts, auction drafts, or DFS salary caps.
  • Stat Tracking- Access to advanced data, projections, and real-time updates.
  • Customization- Ability to tailor scoring systems and roster formats.
  • Mobile App Quality- Season-long leagues demand strong mobile support.
  • Community- Larger platforms mean more active leagues and competition.
